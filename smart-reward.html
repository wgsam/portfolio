Smart Reward

Internal Multilevel Smart Reward E-commerce Platform

01. Overview

In this project, I took on the role of UI/UX designer and developer for "Smart Reward", a multi-level member reward e-commerce platform. It was designed to boost organizational loyalty and user engagement through a unique rewards system, tailored for the Southeast Asian and Malaysian markets. It integrates a dual-wallet system and a referral program and commission across mobile and web platforms for a seamless shopping experience.

Client
Smart Reward - IsCity Sdn Bhd
Role
UI/UX Design, Mobile App Design & Web App Design, Front-end Developer
Timeline
May 2020 — May 2021
Tools

02. Main Challenges

The primary challenge was to effectively integrate a multi-level member reward system into the e-commerce platform to motivate user engagement and loyalty. Existing platforms lacked the ability to incentivize users beyond basic transactions, necessitating a solution that could seamlessly blend commerce with rewards. Traditional loyalty programs failed to incentivize long-term user participation, leading to high customer churn rates and missed opportunities for organic growth.

03. Understand the problem

Background of the Problem:

In the context of an internal organization, particularly within a multi-level structure common in Southeast Asia and Malaysia, the existing e-commerce solutions fell short in several key areas. Traditional platforms were not designed with the unique dynamics of internal organizational use in mind. They lacked features that could foster community, reward loyalty, and facilitate easy management and tracking of multi-level member benefits. Specifically, the challenges included:

User Research:

To deeply understand these challenges, we conducted small group discussions and one-on-one interviews with members of similar multi-level organizations. We aimed to uncover the pain points in their current e-commerce experiences. Key findings included:

Pain Point 1:
Members valued a sense of community and wanted the platform to reflect their organizational structure and culture.
Pain Point 2:
There was a strong desire for a more intuitive way to manage and spend rewards earned through organizational activities.
Pain Point 3:
Members expressed frustration with the difficulty of navigating existing platforms to track their referrals and rewards.

04. Goals

After extensive discussions with our business clients and a deep dive into the needs and aspiration of our target audience, we've crafted a set of goals for "Smart Reward" that serve as our north star. The goals divided into two parts: business goals and user goals. Based on these insights, the goal for 'Smart Reward' was crystallized: To design a bespoke e-commerce platform that not only addresses the unique needs of an internal multi-level organization but also enhances engagement and loyalty through a user-centered design. This platform would:

Business Goals:
  • Create a platform that enhances member engagement and loyalty through a user-friendly reward system.
  • Implement a dual wallet system for efficient reward tracking and management.
  • Leverage the referral system to foster organizational expansion and community building.
  • Seamless integration across platforms
User Goals:
  • Offer a clear and straightforward way for members to manage rewards and referrals, reflecting the organizational structure.
  • Ensure effortless navigation across the platform, enhancing user satisfaction.
  • Strengthen the sense of belonging by integrating the organization's culture into the platform.
  • Offer a secure, reliable shopping environment with flexible payment options, including the innovative dual wallet feature.

08. Project Reflection

What's Next?

The successful launch and user adoption of 'Smart Reward' mark just the beginning of our journey. The insights gained through this project underscore the importance of continuous iteration and responsiveness to user feedback. Moving forward, my immediate focus will be on monitoring user interactions with the platform, gathering feedback, and identifying areas for improvement. Enhancing the user experience remains a top priority, and I plan to delve deeper into data analysis to uncover user behavior patterns that can inform our next series of design improvements. Additionally, staying abreast of the latest UI/UX trends and technological advancements is crucial for ensuring our platform remains innovative and competitive.

What we learned

In this project, I served as the core UI/UX designer, contributing expertise across mobile and web design and overseeing the development process from start to finish. Through this experience, I learned the importance of seamless integration between design and development, ensuring optimal user experience at every stage.

  • Comprehensive Design Scope:
