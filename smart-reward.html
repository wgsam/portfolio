Smart Reward

Multi-level member reward e-commerce platform

01. Overview

In this project, I took on the role of UI/UX designer and developer for "Smart Reward", a multi-level member reward e-commerce platform. It was designed to boost organizational loyalty and user engagement through a unique rewards system, tailored for the Southeast Asian and Malaysian markets. It integrates a dual-wallet system and a referral program and commission across mobile and web platforms for a seamless shopping experience.

02. Main Challenges

The primary challenge was to effectively integrate a multi-level member reward system into the e-commerce platform to motivate user engagement and loyalty. Existing platforms lacked the ability to incentivize users beyond basic transactions, necessitating a solution that could seamlessly blend commerce with rewards. Traditional loyalty programs failed to incentivize long-term user participation, leading to high customer churn rates and missed opportunities for organic growth.

03. Goals

After extensive discussions with our business clients and a deep dive into the needs and aspiration of our target audience, we've crafted a set of goals for "Smart Reward" that serve as our north star. The goals divided into two parts: business goals and user goals.

Business Goals:
To create a platform that drives sales through a unique referral system, improves customer loyalty, and distinguishes itself from competitors by offering a localized shopping experience.
  • Establish the premier multi-level member reward e-commerce platform in our target market
  • Enchance user engagement and loyalty
  • Drive platfrom growth through referrals
  • Seamless integration across platforms
  • Boost transaction frequency and value
User Goals:
To provide users with an enjoyable, secure, and covenient online shopping experience with the rewarding thier participation and loyalty through an innovative rewards system.
  • Intuitive, seamless shoopping experience
  • Rewarding referral system
  • Easy accessibility and use
  • Targible value through cashback rewards

04. Problem

Before the development of "Smart Reward" commenced, we encountered several challenges that needed addressing to ensure the sucess of the platfrom:

1）Fragmented User Experience Across Platform

Research indicated that users were frustrated with e-commerce platforms offering inconsistent experiences between mobile and web. Users found it cumbersome to switch between devices, leading to a disjointed shopping journey.

2）Overwhelming Product Navigation

User feedback from existing platforms highlighted the challenge of navigating through an extensive product catalog without effective sorting or filtering options. This often led to a frustrating shopping experience, with users unable to find desired products quickly.

3）Referral System Utilization

While referrals were recognized as a powerful tool for growth, many platforms did not fully leverage this. The challenge was creating a referral system that was easy to use, transparent, and rewarding enough to encourage genuine user participation for the multi-level platform.

05. Process

Research

Starting the journey of creating a design system, I lacked experience in organizing components and documentation. To gain insights, I studied design systems like Material Design, IBM Design Language, Bootstrap, and Ant Design. Though informative, these systems were extensive and offered more inspiration than practical guidance.

To address this, I embraced Atomic Design principles , which helped structure design elements into reusable components, providing a solid framework. This research deepened my understanding and equipped me with practical frameworks, setting a strong foundation for the project ahead.
