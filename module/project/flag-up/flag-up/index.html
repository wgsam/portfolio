Who We are

Flag Up! App is a charity platform developed by ISCiTy Sdn Bhd that simplifies the process of charity activities.

Individuals and organizations can identify the needies in their area, manage and distribute the resources more evenly and effectively.

Due to increased severity of the pandemic, indefinite closure of economy has unfortunately pushed many Malaysians to their breaking points.

With Flag Up!, we can now assist the needies in just a few simple steps. Flag Up! will serve as a one-stop app for anyone in need or concerned Malaysians willing to help others. From finding out nearby flag locations to navigation, we’ve got you all covered!

With our combined efforts, we will make Malaysia a better place. Be a part of our community and join us today!

OBJECTIVE

A platform to manage the charity activities, and enable even distribution of resources.

MISSION

Helping the poor and hungry to survive

VISION

To become a worldwide charity platform.
