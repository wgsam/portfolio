About me in 10 seconds...

Hey! I'm Sam, UI/UX Designer

My journey into UI/UX design began in front-end development. Over 7 transformative years, I evolved from a developer into a UI/UX Product Designer, gaining expertise in user research, crafting workflows, and designing solutions across fintech, e-commerce, entertainment, and Web 3.0.

Driven by a belief in the impact of exceptional UI/UX, I bring a data-informed perspective to each project, with strengths in responsive design for web and mobile, interactive prototypes, wireframing, design system and front-end development.

I’m passionate about solving complex problems and creating seamless, beautiful solutions that make life easier. Seeing users interact with and enjoy the products I’ve designed is incredibly rewarding.

Outside of design, I find inspiration in photography , capturing stories and moments that spark creativity.
